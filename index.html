WebAbout

Ballians, Inc. is an Indian multinational association that is involved in the design, development, manufacturing and worldwide marketing and sales of apparel, footwear, accessories, equipment and services. The company's world headquarters are situated in Uttar Pradesh, Ballia, in the India. It is a major producer of sports equipment and one of the world’s largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel. It employs more than 44,000 people worldwide and in 2014 the brand alone was valued as $19 billion (€17,5 billion), making it the most valuable brand between sports businesses.Founded by Hareram Singh and Ankush Singh on January 25 1964,
